## VIDEO 3: I Bought a Humanoid Robot: It's Not What You Think

**Tone:** Tech-Futurism, Humorous, Existential.

**HOST:** "For the next 48 hours, I am not lifting a finger. This robot has to run my entire life. If it fails, I don't eat. If it wins... the world is about to change forever."

**HOST:** "China is currently winning the price war. Companies like Unitree are selling entry-level humanoids for just $16,000. That’s cheaper than a mid-range SUV. We are witnessing the 'Hardware Singularity.'"

**VISUAL:** Host at a factory or warehouse (B-Roll).
