**HOST:** "Let’s track where your Lakh goes. Step 1: **The Taxman.** Income tax and GST on everything you touch takes the first 25%. Step 2: **The Landlord.** Rent in a Tier-1 city takes another 30%. Step 3: **The Bank.** Your car EMI and that 'Easy' personal loan take 20%."

**[VISUAL: Host is left with a tiny sliver of the circle. Overlay text: ₹25,000 LEFT.]**

**HOST:** "You have ₹25,000 left for food, fuel, school fees, and parents. And then comes the **'Stealth Tax'—Inflation.** In 2026, food inflation in India is hitting 8%. That ₹25,000 feels like ₹12,000 did in 2019. The middle class is being squeezed from both ends. We are working 60 hours a week just to maintain a 'Standard of Living' that we don't actually own."

**HOST:** "The 'Hidden Agenda' of modern finance is to convert every 'One-Time Purchase' into a 'Lifetime Liability.' You don't own your car; you rent it from the bank.
