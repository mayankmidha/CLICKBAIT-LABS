### [0:45 - 2:30] THE DEEP DIVE CONTEXT

**HOST:** "To understand the collapse, you have to understand the 'Search Tax.' For 20 years, Google trained you to be a researcher. **[VISUAL: Graphic comparing ChatGPT Search market share (65%) vs. Google Gemini Search (21%) in the conversational AI segment.]**

**[VISUAL: Screen recording of the S26 'Proactive Search'. The user takes a photo of a plant. The AI doesn't give links; it gives a full care-guide, pricing at local nurseries, and an 'Order Now' button.]**

Google tried to fight back with Gemini, but they have a problem their competitors don't: **The Ad-Revenue Trap.** If Google gives you the answer directly, they can't sell you the link. They are literally too rich to innovate."

**[VISUAL: Time-lapse. Google gives 10 links to generic tax blogs. The user has to read all of them. Perplexity generates a 3-column table with real-time exchange rates and a final recommendation in 4 seconds.]**

The scariest data point? 70% of Gen Z users now start their search on TikTok or an AI agent, not on a search bar. Google has lost the next generation."

### [7:30 - 9:00] THE ULTIMATE REVEAL: THE WEB COLLAPSE
**[VISUAL: Host in a futuristic server room. The 'Links' connecting the servers are breaking.]**

**HOST:** "But if Google dies, the internet as we know it might die with it. This is the 'Hidden Crisis' of 2026. Small publishers, blogs, and news sites are losing 70% of their traffic because AI 'scrapes' their content and gives the answer without giving the click. No click means no ad-revenue for the creator."

**[VISUAL: Infographic: 'The Scraper Economy'. AI models eat 100% of the data but provide 0% of the traffic back.]**

**HOST:** "We are moving from an 'Open Web' to a 'Synthetic Web.' If the creators stop getting paid, they stop creating. If they stop creating, the AI runs out of data to scrape. We are witnessing the beginning of the **Internet Ouroboros**—the snake eating its own tail. Google is panicking because they are the ones who built this house of cards, and now they’re being buried under it."
